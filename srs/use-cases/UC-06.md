# UC-06: View Product Details

## Use Case ID
UC-06

## Use Case Name
View Product Details

## Primary Actors
Buyer

## Preconditions
- Product exists and is approved.

## Postconditions – Success
- Product details are displayed.

## Main Flow
1. Buyer selects a product.
2. System loads product page.
3. System displays images, title, price, description, and reviews.
4. Buyer views seller details.
5. Buyer may add item to cart.

## Alternative Flows
### AF-01
If product unavailable, system displays out-of-stock notice.

## Business Rules
- Product page must show specifications and reviews.
