# UC-12: Write Product Review

## Use Case ID
UC-12

## Use Case Name
Write Product Review

## Primary Actors
Buyer

## Preconditions
- Buyer completed purchase.

## Postconditions – Success
- Review published.

## Postconditions – Failure
- Review rejected.

## Main Flow
1. Buyer opens delivered order.
2. Buyer selects Leave Review.
3. System shows review form.
4. Buyer enters rating and review.
5. System validates review.
6. Review saved.
7. Rating recalculated.

## Alternative Flows
### AF-01
Review body too short.

## Business Rules
- One review per product per order.
